Palette AI

Turn any image into a color palette!

ThreadsAI is an app designed to assist the visually impaired by recognizing colors, as well as aid the sighted by generating palettes of color that you can use to coordinate your outfits so you look amazing every time. Use your imagination and use ThreadsAI for any purpose you want!

Just follow three easy steps:

  1. Submit the address of the image that you want a palette based on.
  2. Preview the colors that are extracted from the image. The closest color names are provided for people using assistive technologies, and samples are provided for the sighted.
  3. A set of color palettes are generated for each of the dominant colors in the image foreground. Multiple palette styles are provided using this format. All colors should play well together and are recommended for creating your style!

It's that easy!
