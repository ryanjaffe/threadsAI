Coordinating outfits just got easier!

ThreadsAI is an app designed to generate palettes of colors that you can use to coordinate your outfits so you look amazing every time.

Just follow three easy steps:

  1. Take a photo of the clothing that you want a palette based on.
  2. Preview and approve the photo that the colors are extracted from.
  3. Choose a color scheme that the app will use to generate colors.

What color scheme would you like?

Monochrome: A color scheme based on one color with white or black added to create an elegant look, even when a bold color is used.

Analogic: This scheme uses colors adjacent on the color wheel to create a clean look that is either warm or cold.

Complimentary: This scheme uses color opposites on the color wheel to create a bold style.

Analogic-Complimentary: This scheme offers a combination of warm and cold colors creating a bold style with a sense of harmony.

Triad: This scheme is based on three colors equal values apart on the color wheel. No color is dominant in this vibrant look. A bold style for bold people, this is the most colorful palette.

Quadratic: This scheme is based on four colors in complimentary pairs, two warm and two cold. This results in a very vibrant but coordinated look.
